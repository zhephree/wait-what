The Stand-up Comedy Drinking Game

Hosted by Isaac Kozell and Geoffrey Gauchet, “Wait, What?” takes 5 comedians and displays 5 unique drinking cues based on the comedian’s jokes and physical mannerisms. Any time a cue comes up, the Designated Drinker on stage will mark it on the list and we all take a drink!
